Employee Attendance

Web based attendance is available at present and soon will be replaced by bio-metric or any other advanced technical systems will be placed. Branch wise, time wise attendance marking and tracking is applied in this page.
An employee can verify his attendance on day-wise, week-wise, month-wise and year-wise is allowed only for current year.
Overtime working hours tracking & management is facilitated.

View Roster

View Roster page is meant for knowing about employee job and working location with timings.

Apply Leave

All eligible leaves can be requested with Add Leave & withdraw buttons here.
A mobile number is getting collected for emergency contacts.

Claim Expenses

All staff who are eligible for expenses can submit requests with valid attachments.
Provision has provided to display “Claim Status”.

View Events

All internal events which added by Admin can be seen in this page.

View Holidays

Public and National Holidays list which are added from Admin side can view here in date-wise.

View Assets

Any asset(Laptop, tablet, Mobile., etc.,) issued by company to employee can be tracked here.

Attendance Monitoring

Higher Officials is allowed to monitor the attendance of his branch and ratify or verify it. He is allowed to view the scheduled timings against punch-in punch-out timings of employees.

Add Events

Admin was authorized to create events that are going to be shared to all or intended staff.

Employee Profile

This page includes Employee Profile, WordPad, FAQ's, Submit employee details and Preview tabs.

Add Day Wise Roster

Branch head or any person who is in equal or higher capacity only be allowed to prepare this table.
Key-in employee details to the table, right from empID, name, Date, Shifts, in-time and out-time are allowed to mark and ratify the said table.

Week Wise Roster

A cumulative statement comprising of all staff members of branch whose attendance schedules ‘for a week’ can be viewed here.

Month Wise Roster

A cumulative statement comprising of all staff members of branch whose attendance ‘for a month’ can be viewed here.
